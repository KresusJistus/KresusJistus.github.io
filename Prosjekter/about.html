What is XD Reviews?

XD Reviews, or Xtreme Digital Reviews, is a user-based game critics website. Anyone can submit an informational review about a game they have played, and rank it by their user experience and enjoyment.

Who can upload reviews to XD reviews?
Anyone can upload a review to the website. It is completely user-based!
What happens if someone uploads a fake or inappropriate review?
The website is regularly surveilled by our moderators, who will delete content that does not belong on the website.
Do i have to give up private information to upload anything?
No, you will only have to use a preferred username to upload. You will remain anonymous.
